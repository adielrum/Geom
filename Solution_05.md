### **Problem Statement**
Draw a diagram illustrating Theorem 10.

**Theorem 10**: Let $QRR'Q'$ be a parallelogram in $E^{2}$ ($\overleftrightarrow{QQ}^{\prime}||\overleftrightarrow{RR^{\prime}}$ and $\overleftrightarrow{Q^{\prime}R^{\prime}}||\overleftrightarrow{QR}$). Let $l$ and $m$ be parallel lines through $Q$ and $R$. Let $l'$ and $m'$ be parallel lines through $Q^{\prime}$ and $R^{\prime}$ . If $X=l\cap l^{\prime}$ and $Y=m\cap m^{\prime}$ then $\overleftrightarrow{XY}$ is parallel to $\overleftrightarrow{QR}$.
